Covid Vaccination Analysis

A Study on the Correlation And Effectiveness of Covid Vaccinations on the Spread of COVID19

Global Covid Case Percentages Per Country

Simply slide the slider at the bottom to select the month and year you would like to analyze.

Global Covid Vaccination Percentages Per Country

Simply slide the slider at the bottom to select the month and year you would like to analyze.

Covid Cases Vs People Vaccinated Comparison Per Country

Click on any country you'd like to examine, and below the map will display a visualization comparison between the number of people vaccinated in that country and the number of positive cases per month.

Vaccination Distribution By Manufacturer

Click any country on the right to display the vaccines that were distributed per manufacturer. Below shows the number of reported Covid cases for that country to determine whether there is any correlation between vaccine types and their effectiveness on Covid.
